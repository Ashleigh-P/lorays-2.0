About

Lo rays are a Glasgow - based modern rock band that blends glitchy synths, pop hooks and powerful guitar riffs - often pairing their music with cyberpunk - inspired visuals.

At the start of 2025 - Lo Rays were named a 'Band to Watch' by BBC Introducing Rock on Radio 1 and nominated for an 'Act of the Year' on BBC Introducing Scotland. Their music has been played regularly by Alyx Holcombe, Nels Hylton and BBC Radio Scotland.

Lo Rays have supported the likes of VUKOVI, North Atlas and ZAND and are known for putting on theatrical live shows with apocalyptic storylines, dancers and visuals on old VHS TVs.

Finnish songwriter Laura Takala formed Lo Rays in 2018 after moving to Glasgow to follow her dream of making music in the footsteps of her heroes Biffy Clyro. She met Scott Bathgate (guitar), Ryan Jones (bass) and Murray Bartle (drums) at a local gig.
