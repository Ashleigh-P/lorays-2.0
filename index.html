About

Lo Rays formed in 2018 when singer-songwriter Laura Takala followed her dreams and moved from Finland to Glasgow to start a band and make music, inspired by her idols Biffy Clyro. Along the way, she met Ryan Jones (Bass), Murray Bartle, (Drums) and Scott Bathgate (Guitar).


Lo Rays tick all the boxes of a captivating live band. Their massive cyber anthems are sure to stay in your head for hours!

Each show features stunning homemade visuals created by Laura, and are delivered on unconventional retro Orion TVs. You can also check out the band's YouTube channel to see the incredible music videos she creates. The band also, with great detail, make all their own props and outfits for each music video, showcasing their incredible creativity.
